## Berachain-Specific Context

### Protocol Terminology

| Term | Meaning | Context |
|------|---------|---------|
| BGT | Bera Governance Token | Non-transferable, earned from gauges |
| BERA | Native gas token | Liquid, tradeable |
| HENLO | HoneyJar reward token | Community rewards |
| Kitchen | Vault interface (Set & Forgetti) | "Cooking" = depositing |
| Henlocker | Legacy staking system | Being deprecated |

### Community Patterns

- High memetic culture (bear jokes, honey references)
- Strong Discord presence
- Active governance participation
- "Proof of Liquidity" understanding varies widely
